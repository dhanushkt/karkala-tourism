About Durga :


Durga is a Village in Karkal Taluk in Udupi District of Karnataka State, India. It belongs to Mysore Division . It is located 39 KM towards East from District head quarters Udupi. 8 KM from Karkal. 324 KM from State capital Bangalore


Durga Pin code is 576117 and postal head office is Kukkundur .


Miyaru ( 3 KM ) , Sheshadri Nagar ( 3 KM ) , Miyar ( 4 KM ) , Marthap Shetty Colony ( 5 KM ) , Karkal ( 5 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Durga. Durga is surrounded by Sringeri Taluk towards East , Udupi Taluk towards west , Beltangadi Taluk towards South , Mangalore Taluk towards South .
